Valve’s Hardware History

The upcoming Steam Machine is Valve’s latest foray into dedicated gaming hardware, following the success of the handheld Steam Deck. The company’s strategy often involves powerful hardware at competitive prices to expand its Steam ecosystem.

Previous "Steam Machines" from third-party manufacturers had mixed success, but this new first-party device is expected to follow the tightly integrated hardware-software model of the Steam Deck, which proved immensely popular with PC gamers.
