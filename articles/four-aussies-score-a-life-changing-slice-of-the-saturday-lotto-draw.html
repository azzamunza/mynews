By PerthNowOct 25, 20251 min read

Four Australians had their lives changed on Saturday night, each winning a staggering $1,556,751.24 in the latest Saturday Lotto draw.

While none of the Division One winning tickets originated from Western Australia, the state did see six local players each secure a significant $13,563.90 prize in Division Two, spreading the good fortune across the country.
