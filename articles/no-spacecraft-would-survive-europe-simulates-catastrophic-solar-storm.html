'No spacecraft would survive': Europe simulates catastrophic solar storm

Europe has conducted its most extreme space weather simulation to date, revealing a scenario so severe that no spacecraft was left unscathed.

The simulation was part of the European Space Agency's (ESA) annual Space Weather Week and was designed to test the continent's readiness for a worst-case-scenario solar storm.
