OpenAI Signs $38 Billion Deal With Amazon

The landmark agreement allows OpenAI to leverage Amazon Web Services and its vast array of Nvidia AI chips, signalling a strategic shift from its previous exclusivity with Microsoft.

BY AITopics

OpenAI and Amazon have formalised a landmark $38 billion agreement, enabling the AI powerhouse to operate its systems on Amazon's U.S. data centres. This multi-year deal grants OpenAI access to hundreds of thousands of specialised Nvidia AI chips through Amazon Web Services (AWS), marking a significant expansion of its computing infrastructure.

The agreement, announced on Monday, represents a pivotal moment for the AI industry and a strategic diversification for OpenAI. By utilising AWS, OpenAI ensures it has the necessary capacity to support its existing tools, like ChatGPT, and to develop future AI technologies.
